# NovaMart — User Personas

## Customer Personas



### Persona 1 — Priya R.
**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Software Engineer  
**Location:** Bengaluru  
**Type:** Urban Shopper  

**Goals**
- Complete a purchase in as few steps as possible
- See genuine verified reviews before buying
- Get reliable delivery tracking

**Pain points**
- Too many steps in checkout flows
- Can't tell real reviews from fake ones
- Payment errors at the last step

**Quote:** "I want to buy in two taps, not twelve."



### Persona 2 — Arjun M.
**Age:** 35  
**Occupation:** School Teacher  
**Location:** Jaipur  
**Type:** Value Seeker  

**Goals**
- Find the best price with no hidden surprises
